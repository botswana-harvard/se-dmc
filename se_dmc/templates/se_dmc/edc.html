Electronic data capture

What is an EDC System?

An electronic data capture (EDC) system is a computerized system designed for the collection of clinical data in electronic format for use mainly in human clinical trials.[1] EDC replaces the traditional paper-based data collection methodology to streamline data collection and expedite the time to market for drugs and medical devices. EDC solutions are widely adopted by pharmaceutical companies and contract research organizations (CRO).

Features Of Our EDCs

# Feature Description
1 Robust Our EDCs offer high performance as well the ability to detect invalid or altered records even when under extreme load with zero downtime
2 Graphical User Interface Our EDCs are web-based giving users the flexibility to use any device they wish with out compromising security
3 Secure Data Storage Sensitive data will always be encrypted in the database to avoid leaking sensitive information to the public and other users
4 Authorization Limit access to specific users through use of permissions (e.g. some users will be able to see specific records while others cannot)
5 Authentication Only registered users can login
6 Audit Trailing Ability to track activities of everyone who is logged in the system
7 Data Validation Our EDCs check to ensure that (only) the permitted sequencing of steps and events is enforced - if necessary.
8 Training & Support We offer training to new users and support to experienced users if they face any challenges
9 Well Documented Our EDC system come with a reference guide for both new and experienced users
