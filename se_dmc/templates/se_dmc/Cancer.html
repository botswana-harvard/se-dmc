Cancer_study (HIV and Malignancy in Botswana)

An observational study of incidence, toxicity of concurrent treatment, and clinical outcomes. The study plans to evaluate the role of HIV in the incidence and outcome of common AIDS-defining and non-AIDS defining cancers in Botswana.

The project has two components:
•prospective observational study of patients treated for malignancy evaluating response and toxicity.
•bolstering the existing cancer registry to evaluate longitudinal trends and cancer risk factors.

Objective:
•The primary aims are to evaluate the important risk factors for cancer in Botswana (including HIV) and describe the response to treatment for patients without HIV, with HIV but not yet on HAART(highly active antiretroviral therapy), and those with HIV and on HAART. see cancer website
